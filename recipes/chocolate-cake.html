Chocolate Cake Recipe

Description

A delicious chocolate recipe that was shared with me by a high school friend that I have since lost contact with - not there was much contact to start. To the long-lost classmate or friend, thank you for this delicious recipe.

Home

Ingredients

  • 750ml of Cake flour
  • 2ml of Salt
  • 80ml of Cocoa powder
  • 10ml of Bicarbonate of soda
  • 500ml of Sugar
  • 125ml of Oil
  • 15ml of Vanilla Essence
  • 25ml of Vinegar
  • 2 cups of Water

Chocolate Sauce

  • 1 tin of Condensed Milk
  • 1tbsp of Butter
  • 2tbsp of Cocoa powder
  • 1tsp of Vanilla Essence

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C and grease a suitable pan.
  2. Mix the bicarbonate of soda into the water in a mixing bowl, then pour in all the ingredients.
  3. Mix the ingredients together.
  4. Pour the mixture into the pan and bake for approximately 35 minutes or until done.
  5. Remove the cake from the oven and begin preparing the sauce.
  6. Heat the ingredients for the sauce over a medium heat until the sauce thickens.
  7. Spread the sauce over the cake.
  8. Enjoy!
