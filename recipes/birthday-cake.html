Birthday Cake Recipe

Home

Description

This is a rather straight-forward ‘birthday cake’ that we usually bake for birthday celebrations. We usually don't decorate it, but when we do we usually make buttercream icing.

Ingredients

  • Melted butter for greasing
  • 200g Butter
  • 330g Caster sugar
  • 1tsp Vanilla essence
  • 4 Eggs
  • 450g Self-raising flour
  • Pinch of salt
  • 175mls Milk
  1. Preheat oven to 160°C, grease pan with butter and line the base with non-stick baking paper.
  2. Beat the butter, sugar and vanilla essence in a large mixing bowl until pale and creamy. Add the eggs one at a time, and beat well after each addition.
  3. Sift togther the self-raising flour and salt. Mix the sifted flour into the butter mixture alternately with the milk in three separate batches of each. Careful not to overmix.
  4. Spoon the cake mixture into the prepared cake pan and bake in preheated oven for 1 hour and 25 minuted or until cooked through.
  5. Decorate with your own choice of icing and enjoy!
